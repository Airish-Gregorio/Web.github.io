about Queen of tears

Regal Sorrow: Queen of Tears

Follows the dizzying crisis and miraculous rebirth of love between Hong Hae-in (Kim Ji-won), the third-generation Queens Group chaebol and department store queen, and Baek Hyun-woo (Kim Soo-hyun), the son of Yongdu-ri and supermarket prince.

As the shadows of family feuds and societal expectations loom large, Hong Hae-in and Baek Hyun-woo must navigate a labyrinth of passion, betrayal, and redemption, where the true power of love emerges from the ashes of their tumultuous pasts.

Can Hong Hae-in and Baek Hyun-woo defy the odds stacked against them and carve out their own destiny, or will their hearts shatter like fragile glass in the ruthless world of the elite?
